# UPDATE4.md — Classic OPM, 2000s Bands, International Oldies & Big Hitters

## Why Oldies Matter

Filipinos are nostalgic listeners. The songs your parents played in the car, the videoke staples at every family gathering, the 2000s banda songs from your high school barkada — these are in people's emotional DNA. If someone picks "Ang Huling El Bimbo" alongside "Paubaya," that's a COMPLETELY different roast than just modern OPM. The AI needs to know the difference.

## Roast Context for Oldies/Classics

Add to the prompt:

```
CLASSIC/OLDIE SONG ROAST CONTEXT:

- Eraserheads listener → they think liking Eraserheads is a personality trait. They say "OPM isn't dead" at every opportunity. If they picked "Ang Huling El Bimbo," they've been processing the same loss since 1995 and they're STILL not over it. They definitely have opinions about which Eheads era is best and will fight you about it at an inuman.

- 2000s banda era songs (Hale, Spongecola, Sugarfree, Kamikazee) → "Your Spotify is basically a high school reunion playlist. You peaked emotionally in 2007 and every relationship since has been compared to that one. Ang laki ng nostalgia mo, na para bang mas gusto mo pang bumalik sa past kaysa mag-move forward."

- Classic OPM ballads (Aegis, Sharon, Gary V) → "You picked a videoke biritan song for an emotional profiling app. You don't have feelings — you have PERFORMANCES. Your love language is belting 'Basang-Basa Sa Ulan' at full volume while your neighbors contemplate calling the barangay."

- Avril Lavigne listener → they had a "punk phase" in 2004 that never actually ended, it just went underground. They still relate to "Complicated" at 30. They're a trentahin who still wears Converse.

- Adele listener → they don't want to get over their ex. They want to FEEL the pain in 4K resolution with Dolby Atmos surround sound. "Someone Like You" has been on their playlist for 15 years and they're not even embarrassed about it.

- Mix of classic + modern songs → "Your emotional range spans 3 decades. You're crying to Sugarfree AND Moira in the same playlist. Hindi ka nag-move on — nag-UPGRADE ka lang ng heartbreak soundtrack."

- My Chemical Romance / emo listener → "You never left your emo phase, you just started wearing business casual over it. You still feel understood by 'Welcome to the Black Parade' and that's both beautiful and deeply concerning for a grown adult."
```
